Self Audit

As teachers, we are required to have a vast knowledge, understanding and skill set of various technologies and content areas in-order to adequately teach and convey the curricular content. With a subject so broad in nature, I have assessed my own knowledge and skills to help identify gaps in my own professional learning. To help me further develop my confidence and professional persona as a teacher.

Whilst I seek to specialize in focus areas surrounding ‘Food Technology’, ’Textiles’ and ‘Woodwork’, in-order to be able to sufficiently meet the demands placed currently upon teachers and have the confidence/ability to fill in for a peer [teacher] or help out where possible. 

All these areas of weakness identified in my professional learning can be improved to meet curricular standards by continuing studying, via completion of university classes and engagement wit in assessment tasks. Undertaking extensive researching with the use of research papers, TEDTALK videos, and documentaries, and engaging in product development/experimentation to truly grasp and understand the extensive nature of these Focus unit areas of Stage 4 Mandatory Technology.
